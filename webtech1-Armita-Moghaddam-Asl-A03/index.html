Welcome to Soul Drift

Experience the electrifying debut album Soul Drift by The Breeze, a groundbreaking alt-R&B group redefining the genre with their innovative soundscapes. The band—featuring Darya Malakouti, Armin Sadeghlou, and Paulo Barros—unites their diverse backgrounds and shared passion for music in a harmonious collaboration.
Drawing inspiration from the soulful melodies of Middle Eastern traditions and the rhythmic depth of Angolan music, The Breeze creates a distinctive fusion that transcends borders. With lush vocals, evocative lyrics, and immersive instrumentation, they invite listeners on a journey of love, self-discovery, and cultural connection.
Soul Drift isn't just an album; it's an experience—a celebration of heritage and creativity that speaks to the heart of R&B while pushing its boundaries. Prepare to be transported by their unique sound, where tradition meets modernity in perfect harmony.

Backstory Of Soul Drift

Immerse yourself in the captivating world of Soul Drift, the debut album from The Breeze—a trio with a vision to reshape the sound of alt-R&B. Combining the haunting elegance of Middle Eastern melodies with the vibrant energy of Angolan rhythms, The Breeze crafts music that feels like a meeting of souls across cultures and time.
Featuring Darya Malakouti, Armin Sadeghlou, and Paulo Barros, the album is an exploration of connection and transformation. Tracks like "Clouds in Motion" and "Velvet Nights" evoke a dreamlike atmosphere, while songs like "Soulful Current" and "Breeze of Forever" celebrate movement, love, and the power of sound to transcend boundaries.
Soul Drift isn't bound by tradition—it's a daring blend of rich heritage and innovative artistry. Let the waves of this album carry you into a realm where music becomes a language of its own, stirring emotions you didn’t know existed.
Step into the breeze, and let yourself drift.
